08 Emergency Guidelines

🚨 WHEN TO CALL 112 (EMERGENCY):

Call emergency services immediately if you or someone you know experiences:

  • Suspected overdose or poisoning
  • Severe allergic reaction (anaphylaxis) — difficulty breathing, swelling, hives
  • Chest pain, sudden numbness, difficulty speaking (signs of heart attack or stroke)
  • Severe adverse drug reactions — unexpected severe symptoms after taking medication
  • Loss of consciousness or unresponsiveness
  • Any situation where you believe there is an immediate threat to life
Kapsul AI is NOT an emergency service. In any medical emergency, close the app and call 112 or go to the nearest emergency department immediately. Do not rely on any app for emergency medical situations.
