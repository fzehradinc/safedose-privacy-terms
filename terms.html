2. Description of Service

SafeDose is a digital health informational application built on Turkey-specific SGK (Social Security Institution) drug intelligence. The Service provides:

  • Drug–Drug Interaction (DDI) Analysis: Automated checking of whether two or more medications are safe to take together, based on recognized pharmacological interaction data.
  • Drug–Food Interaction Warnings: Alerts about foods or beverages that may interact with your medications.
  • Dosage Timing Reminders: Configurable notifications to help users adhere to their medication schedules.
  • Medication Tracker: A personal digital log of medications, dosages, and schedules.
  • Health Profile Management: Optional storage of chronic conditions, BMI metrics, and allergy information to personalize risk analysis.
  • Supplement Recommendations: Informational suggestions for vitamins and supplements based on profile data.
  • Duty Pharmacy Finder: Real-time location of nearest open or on-duty pharmacies.
  • Pill Recognition: Camera-based drug identification feature.

All features are provided for informational and educational purposes only. The Service does not provide clinical care, prescribe medications, or offer individualized medical advice. See Section 3 for our full medical disclaimer.

We reserve the right to modify, add, or remove features of the Service at any time without prior notice. We will make reasonable efforts to communicate material changes that affect core functionality.
